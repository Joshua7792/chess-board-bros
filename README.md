## Introduction

Chess Board Bros is not your ordinary chess game. It combines classic chess gameplay with exciting power-ups that can change the course of a match. Play against AI opponents with varying difficulty levels or challenge your friends in multiplayer mode.

## Game Features

- **Unique Power-Ups:** Discover and use power-ups to add excitement to each game.
- **Single Player Mode:** Challenge yourself against AI opponents of different skill levels.
- **Multiplayer Mode:** Play with friends and family, either locally or online.
- **Customizable Chessboards:** Personalize your gaming experience with different chessboard designs.
- **Realistic Graphics:** Enjoy high-quality graphics and animations.
- **Leaderboards:** Compete for the top spot on the global leaderboard.
- **Sound Effects:** Immerse yourself in the game with sound effects and music.

Rules and Power-Ups
Chess Board Bros follows standard chess rules with the addition of power-ups that can change the game dynamics. Here are some of the power-ups you might encounter:

Teleport: Randomly moves a piece to a new location on the board.
Swap: Exchanges the positions of two pieces on the board.
Super Move: Allows a piece to move in a special way for one turn.
The game features a tutorial section where you can learn more about the rules and power-ups.
